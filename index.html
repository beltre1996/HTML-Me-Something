Tech School

The technical portion of my Air Force career began in San Angelo Texas in Goodfellow AFB. There I had to learn the fundamentals of signals analysis to fulfill my duties as a 1N2C. For the whole 2 month period I was focused on learning and learning my trade, finally earning my money. Tech school turned out to be another version of college; you learn one day and make mistakes the nexts, rinse and repeat. Needless to say, it was a great time and I'm glad I was there.

Fort Meade

Once I arrived at fort meade I had to sit as a "casual" for several months while I awaited my clearance. This was agonizing since I had just learned what was basically another language. I ended eventually forgot everything I learned and was the best secretary I could be for the local legal office. The time away from classified work made me appreciate the work I would finally be able to do once I badged into the vaunted "NSA." Once I was in" the building," I tore it up. Unfortunately, I can't exactly say what I did but I did great things to put "warheads on foreheads". Trust me.

Florida

I grew up in little Bartow, FL. "Home of the champions" they say. We actually were mostly known for being the jail that housed R Kelly following his arrest for urinating on a 14 year old girl. Other than that, Bartow is a quiet town full of people with diverse backgrounds. It helped instill some good values in me and helped me become the man I am today.
